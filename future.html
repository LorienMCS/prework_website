My Future

My gSchool Goals

I've taken various programming and web design classes over the years, but as you can see by looking at this website, my learning hasn't really jelled yet. I have fun with colors and trying different techniques, but mostly I'm just experimenting. My website isn't the least bit modern-looking or mobile-friendly. I am very much looking forward to learning modern web design and programming techniques, and I'm thrilled with how much I've learned already. Two weeks ago I didn't even know what jQuery was, and now I can make it look as if a GIF on my page is being turned off and on at the click of a button. So fun!

My Goals After gSchool

I don't have a specific idea of where I would like to work, other than definitely working somewhere in the San Francisco Bay Area. I would prefer to work in the East Bay, since that's where I live; but I understand that jobs are more likely to be in San Francisco or even Silicon Valley, so we'll see what happens. I look forward to working with some great people doing something involving what I will learn at gSchool; that sounds like an excellent scenario to me!
