My Past and Present

A Peripatetic Childhood

I was born in California. My dad is a professional folk singer (now retired), and I spent part of the first three years of my life traveling from town to town, across the United States, in a van, while my dad played concerts. The rest of my childhood and most of my teenaged years were spent in Cambridge, Massachusetts.

Now I Travel for Fun

Now I live in the San Francisco East Bay Area, but travelling is still a part of my life: I love to travel, often by train or bus, to interesting and beautiful places, like Yosemite National Park. When I'm not travelling, I'm working to pay for future travels (and/or going to school to improve my ability to pay for future travels), and in my free time I enjoy watching movies with my husband, cooking, amusing and being amused by our cats, and gardening.
